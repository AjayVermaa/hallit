Elaan Convention Center

Royal Orchid Resort, Allalsandra Main Road, Yelahanka, Bangalore - 560064

Offering over 18,000 sq. ft. capacity, Elaan Convention Center is one of the largest and the most gorgeous banquet halls in Yelahanka, Bangalore. This venue is especially created for big fat Indian weddings. The space is provided attached with an additional 4,000 sq. ft. dining room and pre-function area of the lawns. Equipped with the latest technology, this wedding venue has got your entertainment needs covered. The venue has an in-house decor team looking after the design of the banquet. At Elaan Convention Center, outside caterers and DJ are allowed. Alcohol consumption is not permitted on the premises.

Elaan Convention Center, Yelahanka, Bangalore is an expansive venue with high ceilings, beautiful crystal chandeliers and articulately done interiors. The venue is perfect for a grand and big fat Indian wedding as it provides an exuberant hall with luxurious interiors which lend an exquisite ambience along with a plush green lawn that is serene. The venue permits to get a caterer of your choice without any royalty charges and the in house decor team will add to the splendor of the venue making it a unforgettable day for you.

  • Venue allows outside caterers. No royalty applicable.
  • Venue does not allow outside decorators.
  • Venue allows outside DJ.
  • Venue does not allows alcohol. No corkage applicable.
