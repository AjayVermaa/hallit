Nandhana Banquet Hall

No:1342, Old Madras Road, KR Puram Circuit, KR Puram, Bangalore

Nandhana Banquet Hall is a banquet hall located in KR Puram , Bangalore .The banquet hall seating capacity is 100 and floating up to 150 can manage.This venue has car parking facility also which can park up to 20 cars. Outside food not allowed at this banquet hall.Events permitted at Nandhana Banquet Hall are Engagement,Wedding Reception,Birthday Party,Get Together Party,Anniversary,Kitty Party,Naming Ceremony.

Nandhana Palace by Nandhini Palace is a Banquet hall located in No:1342, Old Madras Road, Near Kr Puram Circuit, KR Puram, Bangalore. The venue has all the basic amenities to conduct an board meeting,seminars ,wedding,receptions ., .Wifi is available in the banquet hall.Nandha banquets allow both veg and non-veg in the banquet hall.The minimum capacity of the banquet hall is 50 and the maximum capacity is 180.
